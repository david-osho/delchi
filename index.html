Welcome to DELCHI 50/20 - Santorini

Imagine… basking in the resplendent sunset evenings amidst the enchanting white-washed walls and dreamy blue domes of Santorini where each corner whispers a tale of romance…. Well, we welcome you to Santorini!!!!
The jewel of the Aegean Sea, where the sunsets transform the sky into a canvas of vibrant hues and every corner whispers a tale of wonder.
Santorini promises an idyllic blend of relaxation, adventure, and cultural immersion. If you get the chance try to catch a glimpse of the sun setting on the Caldera. Trust me it will be worth it.
Prepare to be swept away by the island's spirit as you settle into your luxurious accommodations and be immersed in the excitement of the weekend ahead.

Essentials to Remember

  • ✈️ Book your flights! Double-check the dates.
  • 🏨 Confirm your hotel reservation to avoid surprises.
  • 🚖 Arrange taxi for hotel transfers via bookings.com or similar.
  • 🛂 Ensure you have your passport, visa (if needed), and travel insurance.
  • 👙 Pack beachwear for soaking up the sun.
  • 👟 Bring comfortable shoes for exploring cobblestone paths.
  • ☀️ Use sun protection to stay safe.
  • 💊 Carry hayfever tablets if needed.
  • 🧥 Pack a light jacket for cooler evenings.
  • 🔌 Bring a power adapter for Greece (Type C, Type E, or Type F).
